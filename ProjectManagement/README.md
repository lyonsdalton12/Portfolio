Problem #1: Inconsistencies and lack of standardized documentation across our IT environment. This led to numerous occassions of confusion, lack of direction without original authors guidance, and overall increased manual overhead. 
Solution #1: Created a series of standardized templates & guidelines from high-level playbooks to granular script templates.
Problem #2: Data security & delegation of duties being unclear.
Solution #2: Created a set of security levels to provide guidance on data access limitations. 



    Documentation Standards File:
        This file was created to provide a way to standardize our institutes documentation while providing critical missing elements such as naming conventions, baseline information that ought to be included at a minimum, and up-to-date timestamps for not only creation but revisions. 

    Play_Runbook Template File:
        This file was created to provide a high-level explanation of a Playbooks purpose and to delineate between Playbooks/Runbooks. Provided a structured approach of a Playbook/Runbook combination adopted by our institute for simplicity. Highlights the baseline requirements for Playbooks/Runbooks such as creation/revision dates, sensitivity level, subject matter experts and systems in use. This high-level overview provides the reader not only a specific granular process but the context on why that process matters, what it affects, and how it affects other systems. This alongside the Dependencies portion of the document can help those attempting to troubleshoot get a quick snapshot of whether or not this specific document is what they are needing for their current issue. 

    Security Levels File:
        A 4 level system with the goal of helping secure both our propietary assets, as well as custodial assets entrusted to us by our clientel. This guideline document helps as a constant but simple reminder of the importance & ever expanding security landscape we face daily. 

    SQLTemplate File:
        A standard SQL Template to provide clarity of purpose, structured pre-construct area for variables being used in the body of the script, as well as Creation/Revision dates & a quick copy paste section to help expedite intial creation of new SQL assets.
